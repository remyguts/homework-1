About Me

I was born in 1978, I grew up in a small town in Central New York. I've been creating since I was 15. My name is Remy Guts and I'm a multi platform artist. From painting, to design, and music, I have imagined and created in many formats and arenas. I work in graphic design, paint, illustration, and concept. My interests include rock n roll, coffee, conversation, and nature. "Life should be viewed from a lens that doesn't exist"
